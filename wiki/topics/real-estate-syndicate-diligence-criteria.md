## Core Diligence Checklist

- Sponsor/operator track record, including full-cycle exits and downturn performance.
- Sponsor alignment, including co-investment or other "skin in the game."
- Litigation, foreclosure, bankruptcy, and reputational history.
- Transparency, reporting cadence, and quality of investor communications.
- Deal business plan, market thesis, underwriting assumptions, debt structure, fees, and reserves.
- Legal and tax review of the PPM, operating agreement, LP rights, liquidity limits, and K-1 timing.

## Scoring Rubric

- Sponsor: 30%.
- Financials and debt: 25%.
- Business plan and market: 22%.
- Fees and alignment: 10%.
- Tax/legal fit: 8%.
- Macro fit: 5%.

## Red Flags

- Aggressive underwriting or exit assumptions.
- Risky floating-rate debt without adequate caps or reserves.
- Weak sponsor transparency or incomplete reporting.
- Refusal or inability to accept a C-Corp investor where that structure is required.

## Sourcing Channels

Potential channels include CrowdStreet, EquityMultiple, RealtyMogul, Yieldstreet, direct sponsor relationships, 506(c) offerings, 506(b) relationship networks, sector-specialist managers, LP communities, and real estate conferences.
