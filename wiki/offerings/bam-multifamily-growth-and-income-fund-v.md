## Interpretation

The IRR and equity multiple are forward-looking sponsor targets, not realized or guaranteed net investor returns. "Up to 8% preferred returns" is incomplete without accrual, compounding, catch-up, hurdle, distribution, and waterfall terms. Claimed tax benefits also depend on the final structure, taxable income allocations, depreciation policy, investor type, and actual operations.

The captured page does not disclose enough information to assess leverage, cash yield, hold period, portfolio assets, fees, sponsor promote, liquidity, downside protection, or whether targets are gross or net.
