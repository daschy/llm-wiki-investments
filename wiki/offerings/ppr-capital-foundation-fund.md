## Interpretation

The webpage presents two apparent return mechanics, but it does not explain whether they are alternative classes, investor elections, or separate products. The listed returns are sponsor-stated marketing claims, not verified net investor outcomes. The evergreen language says nothing about redemption gates, suspension rights, notice periods, or transfer restrictions.

The detailed fund page says the strategy blends non-performing loans, multifamily, and build-to-rent assets and claims access to collateralized residential mortgage debt and equity investments in apartment and build-to-rent communities. It also says the fund aims for monthly distributions, limited volatility, and a team with 19 years of fund-management experience. Those claims remain unverified.

The Opportunity Fund II FAQ separately refers to a "Reliant Income Fund" with a similar housing-oriented return profile, so the broader naming family may still need reconciliation.

The page set does not disclose fees, leverage, portfolio composition, distribution source, tax structure, or any downside protection. The housing focus and monthly distribution language require review of the actual governing documents before relying on them.
