# GSP REI

Source status: sponsor public-marketing claims from [GSP REI Income Fund Public Webpage](../raw/gsprei.com/single-family-real-estate-income-fund--passive-monthly-cash-flow-summary.md). No legal documents, financial statements, property records, or independent performance verification was reviewed.

GSP REI is presented as a vertically integrated residential real estate sponsor operating [[GSP REI Income Fund]]. The offering should be reviewed using [[Real Estate Syndicate Diligence Criteria]].

## Strategy Claims

- Acquire distressed and value-add single-family homes primarily in Pennsylvania, Maryland, and New Jersey.
- Renovate properties for rental or strategic resale.
- Handle acquisition, construction, property management, and asset management in house.
- Provide quarterly financial statements and management review calls.

## Performance And Risk Claims

The sponsor says it has never missed a payment or lost investor principal and describes its team as having decades of residential real estate, construction, and property-management experience. These statements are unverified and the webpage does not identify the measurement period, investor cohort, audited evidence, realized losses, delinquencies, restructurings, or sponsor entities covered.

## Open Verification

- Establish the sponsor's legal entities, ownership, management team, operating history, litigation, bankruptcy, foreclosure, and regulatory record.
- Obtain audited or independently reconciled financial statements and complete investor payment history.
- Verify assets under management, property ownership, realized transactions, leverage, and related-party arrangements.
- Review whether in-house execution produces genuine cost and control benefits after affiliate compensation and conflicts.
