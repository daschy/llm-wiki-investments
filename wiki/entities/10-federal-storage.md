## Industry Benchmark Comparison

Source status: comparison uses public REIT and industry data summarized in [[Self-Storage Industry Benchmarks]].

10 Federal's sponsor-reported exited self-storage returns are materially above public self-storage REIT benchmark returns. The reported 10FSSAC1 and 10FSSAC2 realized IRRs of 25.75% and 34.85% would be exceptional relative to the public self-storage sector if they are true net investor-level returns.

Key comparison points:

- Nareit self-storage sector data showed 2025 total return of -9.98% and Q1 2026 same-store NOI growth of 0.6%.
- Nareit's August 2024 sector snapshot showed one-year return of 34.72%, three-year return of 5.51%, and five-year return of 11.60%.
- Public Storage's 2025 same-store revenue was roughly flat and same-store NOI declined 0.5%, with weighted-average same-store occupancy of 92.0%.
- Extra Space Storage's 2025 same-store revenue increased 0.1% and same-store NOI declined 1.7%, with ending same-store occupancy of 92.6%.
- CubeSmart's 2025 same-store revenue declined 0.5% and same-store NOI declined 1.1%, with period-average same-store occupancy of 89.7%.

Interpretation: 10 Federal's historical and target returns should be underwritten as private value-add performance claims, not stabilized industry base-case returns. The broader 2025-2026 industry benchmark appears stable but muted, with roughly flat same-store revenue, low or negative same-store NOI growth at several large public operators, and occupancy generally in the high 80s to low 90s.
