## PPR Capital Management Overview

PPR Capital Management presents two current real-estate fund offerings and invites prospective investors to schedule a call with Investor Relations. The public webpage suggests a housing-focused, depreciation-oriented real-estate platform, but the exact legal issuer names, entity structure, and offering status were not reviewed.

## PPR Capital Foundation Fund

The Foundation Fund section describes a diversified housing-focused portfolio for accredited investors. The page shows a 36-month horizon and two apparent return options: an 11% compounding preferred return or a 9% preferred return distributed monthly. It also says the structure is evergreen, has no arbitrary exit date, and accepts qualified plans. The intro language refers to a "Cornerstone Fund," so the exact legal name and whether Cornerstone and Foundation are the same product require confirmation.

## PPR Capital Opportunity Fund II

Opportunity Fund II is presented for qualified purchasers with a 16%+ targeted return over a projected 60 months. The page says the fund seeks shared upside and potential tax benefits through accelerated depreciation. A footnote says the target is based on market intelligence from similar assets that allegedly achieved a 16x-26x exit multiple. These are sponsor-stated targets and analogies, not verified outcomes.
