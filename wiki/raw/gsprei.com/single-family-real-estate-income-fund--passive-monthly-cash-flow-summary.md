## Strategy Claims

The sponsor says the fund acquires distressed and value-add single-family homes primarily in Pennsylvania, Maryland, and New Jersey, renovates them, and either holds them as rentals or sells them. Acquisition, construction, property management, and asset management are described as handled in house.

The webpage characterizes returns as fixed and the investment as designed for predictable income and principal preservation. It also says the sponsor has never missed a payment or lost investor principal. These claims are unverified and do not establish a guarantee, collateral coverage, seniority, liquidity, or future performance.

The webpage attributes to the sponsor's CPA an opinion that the structure does not generate UBIT for self-directed IRAs. The opinion itself and the facts, assumptions, and legal structure supporting it were not provided; qualified tax review is required.
