# 10 Federal Track Record Workbook - 2025-11

Source file: `10 Federal Track Record.xlsx`

Workbook sheets: `Disclaimer`, `10 Federal- Track Record`, and `TE Use`.

Source status: sponsor-provided workbook. It reports a track record as of November 2025 and states that IRRs are project-level IRRs calculated using Excel XIRR from cash equity invested, subsequent contributions, distributions, refinance proceeds, and disposition proceeds. The workbook cautions that figures are best-efforts and should not be relied on for investment decisions.

## Extracted Scope

- The workbook lists exited properties across multifamily, student multifamily, warehouse/land, and self-storage.
- It includes property address, type, owning vehicle, units, purchase date, purchase price, sale date, sale price, percent increase, IRR, equity multiple, sponsor capacity, sponsor co-invest percentage, deal type, and comments.
- Several entries include comments explaining missing or non-comparable IRR/equity-multiple data.
