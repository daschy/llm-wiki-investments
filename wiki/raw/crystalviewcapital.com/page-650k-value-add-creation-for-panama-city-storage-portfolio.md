### $650K Value Add Creation for Panama City Storage Portfolio

At Crystal View Capital, we’re all about creating value from day one—and then going beyond. While we often talk about the initial steps we take within the first 30 days of acquiring a property, our real value-add work doesn’t stop there.

Our team is always on the lookout for new opportunities to unlock even more value. A recent success story comes from our storage portfolio in Panama City, FL, where we were able to generate approximately $650,000 in value. Here’s how:

- Portfolio consists of three properties featuring 413 storage units and 9 commercial spaces, all located just 5-10 minutes apart.
- Through close collaboration between our asset management and field team, we optimized our service contracts with our call center and landscaping services, which resulted in decreasing operating expenses by $13,000 over the past six months.
- We also saw a chance to bring commercial rents up to market rates, further boosting value.

![](https://framerusercontent.com/images/pbfxqCsxcNPw3iwyex56IyA0SAY.webp?width=1480&height=832)

What’s exciting is that this value-add approach isn’t unique to Panama City—we take this hands-on strategy across our entire portfolio. And as we continue raising capital for Fund IV, we’re inviting investors to be part of more success stories like this.

If you’re looking to invest in a proven value-add strategy, connect with our team!
