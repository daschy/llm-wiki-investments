### Newly Acquired! Projected 16.5% IRR with The Latest MHC Acquisition for Fund IV

We’re excited to share the closing of our latest acquisition—**a 103-site mobile home community in southeast Iowa.** This acquisition continues our primary focus on acquiring mobile home communities in Fund IV, reinforcing our commitment to value-add opportunities in the sector.

**Deal Highlights:**

- Sourced off-market
- Average occupancy of 93%
- Align rents with market while maintaining affordability for current and prospective residents - rents currently 45% below market
- Focus on occupancy growth by filling one site per year over the next six years

Projected Returns:

- 16.5% IRR
- 2.1x MOIC
- 10% Cash-on-Cash by Year 6

If you're interested in learning more about this deal and upcoming deals within our acquisition pipeline, [**reach out to our team**](../investor-relations-schedule-call).

For more updates on our acquisition pipeline, [**follow us on LinkedIn**](https://www.linkedin.com/company/crystalviewcapital).
