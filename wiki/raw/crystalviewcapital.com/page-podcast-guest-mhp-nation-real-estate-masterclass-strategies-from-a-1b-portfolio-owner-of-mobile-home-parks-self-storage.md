### Podcast Guest: MHP Nation - Real Estate Masterclass: Strategies from a $1B Portfolio Owner of Mobile Home Parks & Self Storage

Guest: MHP Nation - Real Estate Masterclass: Strategies from a $1B Portfolio Owner of Mobile Home Parks & Self Storage

Watch and listen to hear Matt discuss our investment strategy, focusing on underperforming or non-performing self-storage and mobile home park properties throughout the US. He shares his successful acquisition strategy, the importance of understanding local markets, and the company's strategy for acquiring and restructuring mobile home parks. Also discussed are the risks and potential rewards of investing in real estate assets, the structure of their fund, and the underwriting process for potential deals.
