### Matt's Site Visit to 110-Site MHC in South Carolina Under Contract

Matt Ricciardella just completed a site visit to our latest acquisition, a 110-site mobile home community in South Carolina, set to close in the next 30-60 days.

**Deal Highlights:**

- **Projected ~20% IRR\*** over a 6-year hold
- **Rents 50% below market**, with immediate upside by aligning rents to market
- **Continue occupancy growth**by filing existing sites and potentially expanding the community

Matt’s visit helped us further evaluate the opportunity and confirmed the potential for long-term value creation.

**With market uncertainty continuing, now is the time to invest.** Capital is being admitted in the order it’s received, so if you’re considering an investment, **act now** to secure your position. This opportunity allows for efficient capital deployment into recession-resilient assets that offer strong growth potential.
