### 2024 End of Year Recap: Message from Matt Ricciardella

As we approach the end of the year, we’re proud to share a special message from our Founder and Managing Partner, Matt Ricciardella. In this exclusive year-end recap, Matt reflects on Crystal View Capital’s key accomplishments in 2024, the challenges we overcame, emerging trends in the manufactured housing and self-storage sectors, and what lies ahead for 2025. This year’s success would not have been possible without your trust and partnership. Together, we’ve achieved significant milestones, and we’re deeply grateful for your continued support. As we look to 2025, we’re excited to build on this momentum and create even greater opportunities for success.
