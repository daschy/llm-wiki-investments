### Supporting Our Sarasota & Punta Gorda Communities After Hurricane Milton

In the recent wake of Hurricane Milton, we wanted to provide an update regarding our properties that were in the path of the storm.

First off, our thoughts are with everyone in Florida, specifically Sarasota, Punta Gorda, and surrounding areas as we begin the process of recovery.

We took every precaution to ensure our properties were prepared for the storm. Our on-site management teams worked around the clock, securing facilities and implementing emergency plans to safeguard both property and personnel. We’re pleased to share that due to these efforts, our sites have sustained minimal damage, and all our team members are safe.

![](https://framerusercontent.com/images/wuWG7iD9fvKXapEkPeLWOj7gg4.png?width=1400&height=960)

As we move forward, our focus now shifts toward supporting the local community. We are working closely with local officials to assess immediate needs and provide resources where we can. This includes offering temporary storage solutions for those who may have lost property or need additional space during the rebuilding process, and collaborating with local charities to provide aid to those affected.

We remain committed to ensuring the safety and well-being of our team members, tenants, and neighbors as we work through this challenging time.

These efforts exemplify our commitment to making a positive impact across our portfolio. Investing in our current offering, Fund IV not only supports our growth but also strengthens our ability to give back to communities in need. If you would like to learn more about Fund IV, please visit our website: <https://www.crystalviewcapital.com/fund-iv>

Thanks,

Crystal View Capital
