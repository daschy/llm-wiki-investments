### Turning Returns into Real World Impact

At Crystal View Capital, we believe investing isn’t just about financial yield; it’s about leveraging capital to generate meaningful change. That’s why we’re proud to highlight our partnership with one of our valued investors, Brad Heckerman, founder of the [**God Forgives Foundation**](https://godforgivesfoundation.com/)(GFF).

Brad directs all distributions from Crystal View Capital to the God Forgives Foundation and its affiliated initiative, Angel House, organizations focused on building safe, secure orphanages in developing countries. GFF’s mission is to provide children and broader communities with essentials like shelter, clean water, and the resources they need to thrive.

This partnership is a clear example of impact investing in action. Through disciplined, strategic investment and predictable, consistent distributions, Brad can reliably support these initiatives while pursuing strong financial returns.

Why This Matters

- We see capital as a tool for societal good, not just profit.
- With investors like Brad, the financial model supports both yield and mission.
- By aligning our investment infrastructure with philanthropic flows, we help scale social impact without compromising financial discipline.
- The result is children and communities receive stable support, and partners like GFF can plan for long-term growth.

At Crystal View Capital, we’re proud to partner with investors who turn capital into lasting change.

If you’re seeking not just dollars, but impact, [**let’s talk about how your investments can do more**](../investor-relations-schedule-call), together.
