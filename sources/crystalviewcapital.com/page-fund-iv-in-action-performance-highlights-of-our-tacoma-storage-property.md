### Fund IV in Action: Performance Highlights of Our Tacoma Storage Property

Tacoma, WA isn’t an easy market. With high saturation and plenty of competition, standing out in self-storage takes more than just a good location — it takes thoughtful execution and a team that knows how to move the needle.

That’s exactly what we’ve seen at **Sales Road Storage** over the past year.

From **January 2024 to January 2025**, occupancy jumped **13%** — the result of intentional planning, smart execution, and seamless coordination between our on-site, asset management, and marketing teams.

**Smart Strategy in a Saturated Market**

Tacoma is no stranger to self-storage. With a crowded market, we knew we needed a disciplined and efficient game plan. Our team doubled down on target marketing, smart pricing adjustments, and a hands-on operational approach.

Since **Q3 2024**, the **average rental rate has risen by $5.23**, and with the occupancy gains we’ve seen in **Q1 2025**, the property is now generating an additional **$1,200/month** in revenue.

**Operational Strength Behind the Numbers**

We’ve also seen meaningful improvements on the operations front:

- **Credit loss has been down** significantly since November 2024.
- **Tenant insurance participation** continues to outperform underwriting assumptions, boosting NOI.
- **Controllable expenses** are being actively managed by both our on-site and asset management teams.

![](https://framerusercontent.com/images/lTndZZSdsTLVRIjbR4ZMOuA3ZY.png?width=1280&height=720)

**Curb Appeal + Capacity: A Win-Win**

In 2024, we completed a **curb appeal lift** at Sales Road Storage — including unit repairs, fresh exterior paint, and the removal of outdated landscaping. This not only enhanced the look and feel of the property, but also created space for **additional outdoor parking**, expanding our rental inventory and long-term revenue potential.

**Part of a Growing Portfolio with a Proven Track Record**

Sales Road Storage is just one example of how we create and unlock value within **Fund IV** — which remains open to accredited investors. With a portfolio that includes self-storage and manufactured housing assets, Fund IV is designed to balance cash flow with upside.
