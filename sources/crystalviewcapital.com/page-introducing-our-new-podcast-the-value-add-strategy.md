### Introducing Our New Podcast: The Value Add Strategy

![](https://framerusercontent.com/images/lsVcywDMiOR67D3IVc6WtJRss6I.png?width=3750&height=1969)

We're excited to share that **Crystal View Capital** is launching a new podcast: **The Value Add Strategy.**

This series explores the intersection of business, investing, and personal growth—featuring in-depth conversations with investors, operators, and entrepreneurs who have built meaningful legacies in and beyond the real estate world.

Each episode goes beyond strategy. We dive into the experiences that shaped our guests—uncovering how they navigate opportunity, risk, and the responsibility that comes with success. From value creation and capital allocation to leadership and philanthropy, these are stories worth sharing.
