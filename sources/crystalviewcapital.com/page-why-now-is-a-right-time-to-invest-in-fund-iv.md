### Why Now Is a Right Time to Invest in Fund IV

As year-end approaches, investors in private equity real estate face crucial timing decisions. In this video, Matt Ricciardella explains how Net Asset Value (NAV) evolves as Fund IV acquires and optimizes [manufactured housing communities and self-storage](../strategy) assets—and why early entry can significantly impact long-term returns.

###### Why Enter Fund IV Early Matters

- Early investors participate before NAV fully reflects value creation
- Timing impacts long-term outcomes and potential returns

**Fund IV Is Actively Deploying Capital**

- Assets are already generating income
- Operational improvements are underway to drive growth
- Acquisition pipeline remains strong

**Year-End Is Critical for Investors**

- Entering before year-end allows participation in Fund IV’s growth phase
- Delaying could mean missing the optimal NAV entry point

Watch the video above to see how Fund IV is positioned today and learn why now is the right time to invest. [Explore Fund IV](../fund-iv) before year-end to capitalize on early opportunities.
