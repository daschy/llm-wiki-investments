### 136-Site Northeast Ohio in MHC Closing This Month

![](https://framerusercontent.com/images/3Tqzkrs4gi7rR1Mea8aWxv0.jpg)

We are on track to close on a [136-site mobile home community in Northeast Ohio](https://www.crystalviewcapital.com/page/fund-iv-136-site-ohio-mhc-under-contract) by the end of **December 2025**. With 94% occupancy and rent-to-market alignment opportunity, the asset is positioned for a targeted value-add execution that drives operational performance and long-term value creation.

Following acquisition, we plan to implement submetered utilities, a targeted strategy designed to optimize operations, improve net revenue, and drive strong cash-on-cash returns and IRR for investors.

Watch our Founder, Matthew Ricciardella, on-site at the community for an exclusive update and insights into this acquisition. [Click here](https://youtu.be/vTeciqC8KHU)

This opportunity reflects our disciplined, value-add approach: identifying resilient, income-generating communities with immediate upside. **Capital is being deployed in the order received**, and allocations in [**Fund IV**](../fund-iv)are limited.

**Act now to secure your spot and participate in this acquisition.** [**Schedule a call with our team**](../investor-relations-schedule-call)**.**
