### Q1 2025 Update: Strong Cash Flow and Strategic Growth

At Crystal View Capital, our mission is to create long-term value through disciplined investments in recession-resistant assets. The first quarter of 2025 reflects the strength of our vertically integrated model and our commitment to delivering consistent results for our investors.

##### Cash Flow and Distributions

In Q1 2025, we generated strong free cash flow from operations across all of our Funds, enabling us to make consistent investor distributions:

- **Fund II** issued its scheduled quarterly preferred distribution.
- **Fund III** distributed capital as cash flow from stabilized assets continued to grow.
- **Fund IV** is making a full Q1 distribution and is nearly fully caught up on all deferred preferred returns—an impressive milestone given the fund’s recent inception.

These distributions underscore our focus on acquiring undervalued assets and enhancing their performance to generate stable cash flows.​

##### Acquisition Pipeline Update

We maintain a robust pipeline of off-market real estate deals, underscoring our ability to source attractive opportunities in today’s competitive environment. Upcoming acquisitions include:

- A mobile home community in South Carolina, expected to close within 30 days.
- A multi-asset portfolio in the Pacific Northwest, slated to close in the next 45–60 days.
- A mobile home community in the Southeast currently under contract.
- Additional Midwest-based deals in advanced negotiations.

These acquisitions align with our strategy of targeting mom-and-pop-owned assets in secondary and tertiary markets, minimizing competition and capitalizing on opportunities often overlooked by institutional investors

##### Looking Ahead

As we continue to deploy capital and enhance our portfolio, we remain committed to our core values of transparency, integrity, and hands-on expertise. Our focus on recession-resistant sectors, combined with our proven value-add strategies, positions us to deliver reliable returns and transform communities.​
