### Podcast Guest: Global Investor Podcast - Manufactured Housing and Self Storage Investing with Matthew Ricciardella

Matthew is a seasoned real estate expert with over 20 years of experience and $1 billion in transactions under his belt. As the founder of Crystal View Capital, he specializes in self-storage and mobile home communities, and in this episode, he shares his journey and expertise.

What we'll cover:

- Matthew's background and why he chose real estate
- Crystal View Capital's investment strategy and criteria
- The advantages of self-storage and mobile home communities 🛠️ Challenges in property management and the power of vertical integration
- Off-market deal acquisition strategies
- Financing methods for property purchases
- How investment strategies evolve over time
- Impact of new self-storage supply on the industry
- Common mistakes to avoid in real estate investing
- Matthew’s relationship with money and his keys to success

Whether you're a seasoned investor or just starting out, this interview is packed with actionable insights into the world of real estate investing, especially in niche markets like self-storage and mobile home communities.

Don't miss Matthew's expert advice—learn how to apply these strategies to your own ventures!
