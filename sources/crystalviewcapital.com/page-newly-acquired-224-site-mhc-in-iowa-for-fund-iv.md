### Newly Acquired: 224-Site MHC in Iowa for Fund IV

![crystal view capital mobile home community](https://framerusercontent.com/images/yVQdayqR0JTz67Mq7jhboqXfvow.png?width=1320&height=619)

We’re pleased to announce that Fund IV closed on a 224-site mobile home community located in eastern Iowa.

This community presents multiple levers for value creation that align with our investment strategy:

- **Strong Foundation**: the property has been mom-and-pop owned and operated and is in well-maintained condition.
- **Rent Growth Opportunity**: Current rents are below market, providing room to align with local market levels over time while maintaining affordable housing options for residents.
- **Utility Efficiency**: The property is on unmetered city utilities, allowing us to implement sub-metering to reduce costs.
- **Strategic Market Synergy**: With Fund III already operating a 423-site community in the same market, this acquisition enhances management efficiency, reduces operational costs, and strengthens our regional presence.

Based on our initial underwriting projections, we are projecting:

- 23.9% IRR
- 2.7x MOIC
- 15.5% Cash on Cash by Year 6

This acquisition aligns with our strategic focus on value-add opportunities in the manufactured housing sector. For more details on the community or any of our active investments, feel free to [**schedule a call with us**](https://www.crystalviewcapital.com/investor-relations-schedule-call).
