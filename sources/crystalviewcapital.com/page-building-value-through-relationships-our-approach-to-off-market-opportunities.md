### Building Value Through Relationships: Our Approach to Off-Market Opportunities

At Crystal View Capital, we don’t just acquire assets—we build relationships. Our strategy is simple but powerful: cultivate real, trusted connections with property owners to uncover off-market manufactured housing communities that often go unnoticed by others.

Why focus on relationships? Because understanding what owners need allows us to create solutions that work for everyone. This approach opens doors to high-quality opportunities even in competitive markets, giving us early access to assets that align with our investment thesis.

As institutional interest in manufactured housing grows, our relationship-first model continues to be a differentiator. It allows us to act quickly and strategically, securing properties that deliver long-term growth while maintaining operational excellence.

By leveraging these opportunities, we create meaningful value for our investors—driving results that reflect both strategic insight and hands-on execution.

At Crystal View Capital, relationships aren’t just part of our process—they’re the key to uncovering opportunities that others miss.
