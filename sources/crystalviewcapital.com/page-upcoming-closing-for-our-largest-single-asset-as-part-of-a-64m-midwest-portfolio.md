### Upcoming Closing for Our Largest Single Asset as Part of a $64M Midwest Portfolio

Thanks to the overwhelming response to Fund IV, we have achieved significant growth within our portfolio. With that, we are pleased to share that we expect to close this month on our largest single asset.

This premier asset is part of Phase 2 of our $64 million Midwest portfolio in our current acquisition pipeline. With this deal, we have the opportunity to efficiently deploy capital - which will be admitted in the order received.

Watch the video above to learn more.
