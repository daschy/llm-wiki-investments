### Creating Community and Value: An Impact-Driven Approach in Arkansas

At Crystal View Capital, we believe challenges are simply opportunities in disguise, and our 174-site mobile home community in Arkansas is a testament to that philosophy.

Once facing flooding concerns and underutilized capacity, the property is now nearing full occupancy. With only three vacant lots remaining (one of which is currently under contract), the community has shown steady and impressive progress.

Thanks to disciplined expense management and strong collections, this asset continues to perform consistently. In fact, our on-site team achieves “zero hero” status nearly every month, meaning no delinquent residents, and remains committed to delivering best-in-class customer service.

![](https://framerusercontent.com/images/4f8Udd3bKEHdAFCD2geLEbeodQ.jpg?width=1798&height=1161)

**Recent operational improvements include:**

- Strategic paving projects
- Installation of French drains to address stormwater issues
- A refreshed community room to elevate the resident experience
- A planned 4% lot rent increase in 2025, projected to generate $360,000 in additional annual revenue

This progress underscores how impact investing in essential housing can drive both financial returns and long-term community benefit. Through smart reinvestment and hands-on management, we’re not only improving property performance, we're also enhancing the daily lives of residents.

We’re proud of the performance trajectory of this community and the ongoing value we’re creating across our portfolio.
