### A Closer Look at Our Latest Acquisition Opportunity - 103 Site MHC

At Crystal View Capital, we pride ourselves on a hands-on approach to every investment. Matt recently visited one of our upcoming acquisitions in the Midwest, **projected to deliver a 20% IRR.**

Before any property is added to our portfolio, Matt personally visits to ensure it aligns with our value-add strategy. This commitment to due diligence allows him to identify opportunities, address challenges, and share key takeaways with our asset management team—ensuring seamless execution of our strategy.

In this short video, Matt highlights the value-add potential of this property and why this approach impacts delivering results.
