### Newly Acquired: 136-Site Northeast Ohio MHC for Fund IV

We’re excited to announce that [Fund IV](../fund-iv) has closed on a 136-site manufactured housing community in northeast Ohio. Investor capital is now actively deployed, driving immediate improvements, community growth, and long-term value creation in private equity real estate

###### Deal Highlights:

- **Occupancy**: 94% occupied
- **Upside Opportunities**: Rent-to-market alignment
- **Operational Enhancements**: Implement submetered utilities

###### Watch the Latest Video Update

Matt Ricciardella provides a full update on this acquisition, Fund IV’s additional portfolio activity, and our outlook for 2026. Watch the video above to see how Fund IV is positioned today.

###### Ongoing Deal Flow

- Robust acquisition pipeline in motion
- Capital deployment continues efficiently
- Investor capital admitted in the order received
