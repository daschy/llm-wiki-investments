### Site Visit: 224-Site MHC in Eastern Iowa Under Contract

At Crystal View Capital, we take pride in identifying high-quality investment opportunities that bring strong returns for our investors while maintaining the integrity and pride of ownership in the communities we acquire. Recently, Matt Ricciardella had the opportunity to visit our newest acquisition—a well-maintained, 224-site mobile home community in Eastern Iowa.

During his site visit, he met with the ownership group, a dedicated mom-and-pop operation that has done a phenomenal job maintaining this community. Their commitment is evident in the solid infrastructure: concrete streets, city water and sewer, and the overall great bones of the property.

As we prepare to close this investment within the next 45 to 60 days as part of Fund IV, our strategic plan is clear—bringing rents to a market level, introducing new homes, and carefully recycling older homes to preserve the community’s character and appeal. By enhancing the property and optimizing its potential, we continue to drive value for our investors while ensuring residents enjoy a high-quality living environment.

If you have any questions about this exciting acquisition or would like to learn more about our investment approach, feel free to reach out to us at **invest@crystalviewcapital.com** or [schedule a call with our team](../investor-relations-schedule-call).
