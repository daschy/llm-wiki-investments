### Under Contract! 110-Site MHC in South Carolina Offering a Projected 19% IRR

We just put another manufactured housing community under contract for **Fund IV**—a **110-site community in Florence, South Carolina!**

Deal Highlights:

- Projected 19% IRR and 2.5x MOIC over a 6-year hold
- Rents 50% below market - immediate potential upside with aligning rents to market
- Utilize our in-house home sales team to fill one site per year

If you're interested in learning more about this deal or others we're putting under contract, [**schedule a call with us today**.](https://www.crystalviewcapital.com/investor-relations-schedule-call?utm_campaign=Acquisition%20Pipeline%20Updates&utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz--n5PE9Ha_RZzrMHU0h6ECWphpBrR7cV3F6beu_f542HE3VHeyJaLJspbF9nGbhdzdclhmX)
