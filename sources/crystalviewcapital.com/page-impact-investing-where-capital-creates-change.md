### Impact Investing: Where Capital Creates Change

In a world facing complex social and environmental challenges, more investors are asking themselves a powerful question: *What if my capital could do more than just grow?*

That’s where impact investing comes in.

Impact investing is a strategy that aims to generate not only financial returns but also positive, measurable outcomes for society or the environment. It’s about aligning your money with your values and being intentional about where and how you invest.

Whether it’s supporting affordable housing, renewable energy, or educational access, impact investing allows you to participate in change while still pursuing competitive returns.

But what does this look like in real life?

In a recent episode of the *Value-Add Strategy Podcast*, entrepreneur and investor **Brad Heckerman** shares a personal story that brings the concept to life. After realizing strong returns on his investments, Brad took it one step further: channeling those earnings into philanthropic efforts with his [God Forgives Foundation](https://www.godforgivesfoundation.com/) that is now making a real difference globally.
