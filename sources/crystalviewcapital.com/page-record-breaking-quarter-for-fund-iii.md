### Record Breaking Quarter for Fund III

Fund III just closed out its best quarter yet from a cash flow perspective, reaching 120% coverage over our preferred return—up from 106% in the prior quarter. As a result, **we issued a $600K special distribution to our investors.**

Several properties within the portfolio contributed to this performance, showcasing the effectiveness of our disciplined asset management and value-add strategies.

For more details, we encourage you to watch our latest video update, where we highlight the key properties driving this success.

What we’re doing in Fund III, we’re also executing in Fund IV—creating long-term value and generating strong cash flow performance. If you’re interested in learning more about Fund IV and how you can invest, [click here to explore the opportunity](https://www.crystalviewcapital.com/fund-iv?utm_campaign=6889092-Fund%20Updates&utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz--XD9DdcBh8lF4RzTilFgIU5slYwZ2S50kfnPmL0qy5y-oxKm525UMrEj8XE0h1SreQD_aK).
