## PPR Capital Foundation Fund Overview

The page describes a diversified residential real-estate fund for accredited investors. It says the fund is built on the thesis of "housing people" and offers access to collateralized residential mortgage debt and equity investments in apartment and build-to-rent communities. The page also emphasizes monthly distributions, limited volatility, and a team with 19 years of fund-management experience.

## Strategy And Portfolio Claims

The fund says it blends three asset classes: non-performing loans, multifamily, and build-to-rent properties. It frames NPLs as discounted debt-resolution opportunities, multifamily as cash flow plus appreciation, and build-to-rent as a demographic-driven rental-housing strategy. The page presents the mix as a diversification mechanism across risk profiles, market cycles, and return timelines.
