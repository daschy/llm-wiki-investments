# Our Story

From humble beginnings to a respected name in real estate investment, Crystal View Capital's journey is one of perseverance, integrity, and visionary leadership. Founded by Matthew Ricciardella, a self-made real estate entrepreneur, our firm has grown over the past decade by staying true to our core values, embracing impact investing, and focusing on often-overlooked, recession-resistant assets.

We're not just in the business of returns; we're in the business of transforming communities. Through our investments in self-storage and manufactured housing, we're providing essential services and affordable housing solutions across the country.

## Leadership Team

![](https://framerusercontent.com/images/LTS7LneQPlwGB99MnSMasoWM1g.png?width=1840&height=1750)

Matthew Ricciardella

Principal & Managing Partner

With over 19 years of experience in the real estate industry, Matthew has personally been a principal in over $500 million in real estate transactions. As the founder and CEO, he is responsible for the management and oversight of the Fund, including investment strategy, fundraising, asset management, and operations.

![](https://framerusercontent.com/images/vSpWit6GLOymFMKm4TVdGYj5xlY.png?width=1840&height=1720)

Jessica Stender

Vice President of Operations

With a background in educational operations management, Jessica joined Crystal View Capital in 2021. She is responsible for developing and implementing key operational strategies for over 90 employees, focusing on lean financial operations and abundant productivity.

![](https://framerusercontent.com/images/LSTSxTrteW8JV99aL1eq5If83A.png?width=1840&height=1720)

Ben Brundage

Director of Capital Formation & Investor Relations

Ben joined Crystal View Capital in June 2024 to lead capital formation strategies and strengthen investor relationships. With over a decade of experience in financial services and investor relations, he brings expertise in real estate and a proven track record of cultivating investor confidence.
