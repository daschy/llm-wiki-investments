### The Value Add Strategy Podcast: Episode 2 with Brad Heckerman

In this episode, we sit down with Brad Heckerman, a seasoned investor and philanthropist, to dive into how investment returns are being used to change lives across the globe. From building orphanages in India to creating life-saving opportunities for at-risk children, Brad shares how his faith, values, and passion for people have shaped his investment strategy.

Learn more about the God Forgives Foundation here: <https://www.godforgivesfoundation.com>

Episode Highlights:

How investing in mobile home communities and self-storage is more than just about returns, it’s about making a lasting impact.

The powerful role of integrity and experience in vetting investment opportunities.

How our capital is being used to build orphanages, transform lives, and offer a future to children at risk of trafficking.

The power of faith and stewardship in business—and why it’s a competitive advantage in today’s market.

Join us as we discuss the real purpose behind every deal and how investing for a greater good can shape the future of communities both locally and internationally.

Subscribe to stay updated on future episodes at [www.thevalueaddstrategy.com](https:// www.thevalueaddstrategy.com)
