### Scaling Growth Through Our 2026 MHC Call Center

In 2026, Crystal View Capital is launching a centralized call center to streamline operations and enhance the resident journey across our manufactured housing communities.

## **Why a Call Center**

The traditional on-site model no longer matches resident behavior. Even strong on-site teams cannot always take every call while handling move-ins, tours, and day-to-day operations. Our call center ensures 100% coverage, consistent brand-aligned communication, and a centralized sales pipeline to guide every prospect efficiently from inquiry to homeownership.

## **Driving Conversions with Dedicated Sales Agents**

Trained internal sales agents focus exclusively on lead nurturing. They understand our homes, underwriting process, and lending partnerships, which accelerates prospects from interest to finance-ready buyers. Communities with dedicated follow-up often see 20 to 40 percent higher conversion rates.

## **Data-Driven Decisions and Operational Efficiency**

Centralizing calls allows us to capture demographic and lead data across our portfolio, powering smarter decisions on pricing, marketing, home ordering, and occupancy strategies. It also reduces on-site administrative time by 30 to 50 percent, letting teams focus on move-ins, resident relations, and operations.

## **Enhancing the Resident Experience**

Fast, consistent, and professional communication builds trust, improves follow-up, and ensures every prospect experiences the same high-quality service, no matter the location.

## **Looking Ahead**

Our call center is more than a service. It is a core operational platform that supports growth, improves conversions, and strengthens investor confidence. This initiative positions Crystal View Capital to scale efficiently while maximizing value for residents and investors alike.
