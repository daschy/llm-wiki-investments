### Crystal View Capital Announces New Director of Capital Formation and Investor Relations

Las Vegas based private equity firm Crystal View Capital appointed a new Director of Capital Formation and Investor Relations, [Ben Brundage](https://www.linkedin.com/in/benjamin-brundage/).

![](https://framerusercontent.com/images/Urp9jtbsL06vN7SUXOOL2MjD5vs.png?width=526&height=744)

Ben joined Crystal View Capital in June 2024 and will spearhead Crystal View Capital’s efforts in capital formation strategies and strengthen relationships with investors. With over a decade of experience in financial services and investor relations, Ben brings a wealth of expertise to the firm. His deep understanding of real-estate and proven track record in cultivating investor confidence and serving clients with excellence will play a pivotal role in driving Crystal View Capital’s growth trajectory.

Prior to joining Crystal View Capital, Ben Brundage held key positions in political capital development, real-estate development, and most recently in the private fund space where he successfully executed comprehensive investor relations strategies and contributed to significant fundraising initiatives. His experience in navigating real estate and his commitment to client-centric service align perfectly with Crystal View Capital’s core values.

"I look forward to working closely with the talented team at Crystal View to drive strategic growth across the firm," said Ben Brundage. "It is no understatement to say, the partnership between Crystal View Capital and its investors is invaluable to us. We are dedicated to meticulously serving our investors ensuring that we meet and exceed expectations."

[Crystal View Capital](https://www.crystalviewcapital.com/) is a private equity real estate firm that focuses on the acquisition and management of opportunistic self-storage facilities and manufactured housing communities. The firm creates value by sourcing deals primarily off-market and utilizing their in-house management team to influence property performance. Presently, the firm is raising $200 million in capital for its fourth fund, Crystal View Capital Fund IV LP.

To learn more about our firm and our latest fund, [Fund IV](https://www.crystalviewcapital.com/fund-iv?utm_source=website&utm_medium=news&utm_campaign=director), please visit our website and schedule a call with Ben.
