### Driving Value and Impact: How Crystal View Capital is Delivering Strong Returns

At Crystal View Capital, our focus has always been on creating meaningful value for our investors through disciplined, operationally driven strategies. Our recent video highlights how these strategies are translating into measurable results, including approximately $19.5 million returned to investors through cash-out refinances.

The video showcases our approach to stabilizing assets across five self-storage facilities and one manufactured housing community. By identifying operational inefficiencies and implementing our value-add initiatives, including bringing rents to market, passing through utilities, enhancing curb appeal, and investing in strategic CapEx, we are able to drive NOI growth and create long-term value.

A core component of our approach is vertical integration, ensuring alignment across management, operations, and capital deployment. This model increases efficiency, strengthens performance, and positions our assets to deliver sustainable, predictable returns. In parallel, our commitment to impact investing ensures that the value we create benefits not only investors but also the communities in which we operate.

We are now applying these same proven strategies to new opportunities. Investors have the chance to participate in resilient, high-performing assets that generate strong cash-on-cash returns, future distributions, and increased valuations over time.
