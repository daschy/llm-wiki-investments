### Affordable Housing, Upgraded Amenities, and Lasting Impact in Muskegon

At Crystal View Capital, we don’t just acquire properties—we transform them with a focus on long-term growth and operational excellence.

Our COO recently visited one of our properties in Muskegon, MI, a 223-site community we acquired in February 2024. Over the past year, we’ve made significant strides, including bringing in new manufactured homes to provide affordable housing options and enhancing community amenities, such as upgrading the playground.

**Want to see our strategy in action?** Watch this video of our COO’s recent visit to the Muskegon property and learn more about how we’re transforming communities:

The rising affordability crisis has created an urgent need for reliable housing solutions. Manufactured housing communities like those within our portfolio play a critical role in bridging this gap, offering quality homes at an attainable price point. At the same time, they present an investment opportunity, combining resilience with the potential for strong returns.

By investing in Crystal View Capital, you’re not only contributing to addressing this nationwide challenge but also participating in a strategy built for long-term success. Our hands-on, value-add approach ensures that we deliver both for our investors and the communities we serve.

This is just one example of how we align strategic vision with operational execution to create lasting impact.
