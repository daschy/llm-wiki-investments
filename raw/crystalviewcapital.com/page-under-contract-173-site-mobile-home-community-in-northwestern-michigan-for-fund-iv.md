### Under Contract: 173-Site Mobile Home Community in Northwestern Michigan for Fund IV

Crystal View Capital Fund IV is under contract to acquire a 173-site mobile home community in Northwestern Michigan, continuing to expand our footprint in the Michigan manufactured housing market. This deal was sourced off-market from a mom-and-pop absentee owner.

###### **Deal Highlights:**

- **Local Synergy:** We already own 223 site mobile home community within 10 miles of this property, allowing for efficient, centralized management.
- **Strong Value:** Acquisition price is approximately $39,000 per site, well below replacement cost.
- **Upside in Rents:** Current lot rents: $425, with market rents $600+. Our plan is to gradually adjust rents to market over the next two years.
- **Additional Opportunity:** The community includes 15 vacant homes that weren’t included in our base projections. With successful rehab and lease-up, there’s meaningful upside.

###### **Projected Returns:**

- 7.5% cash-on-cash in year three
- 19–20% IRR over a six-year hold

[Learn more](https://www.crystalviewcapital.com/fund-iv) about Fund IV or [schedule a call with our team](https://www.crystalviewcapital.com/investor-relations-schedule-call) to discuss this latest acquisition in our pipeline.
