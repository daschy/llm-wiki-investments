## Overview

The GSP REI Income Fund is a fixed rate, fixed-term investment designed for passive investors seeking predictable income. Returns range from 8–12% depending on the investment amount and term.

### Distributions

Paid monthly (on the 15th business day) or compounded for higher returns.

### Redemption

At the end of the term, investors may request full or partial redemption, with principal returned within 90 days.

### Eligibility

Suitable for personal accounts, entities, self-directed IRAs, solo 401(k)s, and trusts. Our CPA’s opinion is that this structure does not generate Unrelated Business Income Tax (UBIT) for self-directed IRAs.
